Operations / Financial Modeling

Good investment decisions depend on incredible luck or excellent reference data. The chance of incredible luck is out of our hands, however within every business we can find excellent data. We have years of experience developing detailed financial models for investment projects, showing the likely operational profitability under different variables.

Our models are 'living', i.e. assumption fields can be manipulated with different values, giving instant results of possible scenarios. Additionally, if the project is successful then data from this model can be used as initial budget inputs for both financial and operating data.

As our models detail costs and revenue to the individual unit level, clients find them to be incredibly useful for both understanding exactly which service lines are most profitable (and why), and alternatively which service units are not up to standard (and why).
